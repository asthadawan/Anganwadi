Nutrition Services

Helping Children Grow Strong

Our nutrition program ensures every child receives balanced meals, growth monitoring, and timely supplements. We work closely with families to promote healthy habits that keep children energized and ready to learn.

Daily hot meals, seasonal menu rotations, and locally sourced ingredients help us deliver wholesome nourishment. We also organize nutrition awareness sessions for mothers and caregivers, creating a supportive network around every child.
